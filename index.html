Grammar Guide

دليل شامل لجميع القواعد والأزمنة مع أمثلة كاملة ونصائح واضحة لاجتياز الاختبار

⏰

Verb Tenses — الأزمنة

الأزمنة هي قلب اللغة الإنجليزية. كل زمن له كلمات دلالية تميزه. فهمها = نجاح مضمون في الاختبار.

TenseFormulaKey Signal WordsExample
Present SimpleS + V(s/es)every day, always, usually, never, oftenShe drives to work every day.
Past SimpleS + V-ed / irregularyesterday, last night, ago, in 2020They went to the cinema last night.
Present ContinuousS + am/is/are + V-ingnow, at the moment, currently, Look!He is studying for the exam now.
Past ContinuousS + was/were + V-ingwhen, while, at 5 o'clock yesterdayThey were having lunch when the phone rang.
Present PerfectS + have/has + V3already, yet, never, ever, just, since, for, so farI have never seen such a sunset.
Past PerfectS + had + V3before, by the time, after, already (past)They had finished before the deadline.
Present Perfect Cont.S + have/has been + V-ingfor/since + still ongoing nowWe have been studying for two years.
Future (going to)S + am/is/are going to + Vtomorrow, next week, plansThey are going to the zoo tomorrow.
Future (will)S + will + Vpromises, instant decisions, predictionsI will call you as soon as I arrive.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • She go to school every day.
    ✓ She goes to school every day. (he/she/it + s)
  • I seen the movie already.
    ✓ I have seen the movie. (need have/has)
  • They was playing football.
    ✓ They were playing football. (plural → were)
  • She has went to Cairo.
    ✓ She has gone to Cairo. (V3 not V2)
  • I will call when I will get home.
    ✓ I will call when I get home. (no will after when)
  • He is studying since 2020.
    ✓ He has been studying since 2020.

💡 Quick Rules

  • He/She/It → always add s/es in Present Simple
  • Use was for I/He/She/It, were for You/We/They
  • Present Perfect: have (I/you/we/they) or has (he/she/it)
  • Use for with duration, since with a start point
  • In time clauses (when/if/as soon as) → Present Simple, NOT Future
  • Past Perfect = action done BEFORE another past action
  • Just/already/yet/ever/never → always Present Perfect

Quantifiers — الكميات

Much/Many/Some/Any/A lot of/Few/Little — كل واحدة لها استخدام محدد حسب نوع الاسم ونوع الجملة.

QuantifierUse withSentence TypeExample
MUCHUncountable❌ Negative / ❓ QuestionWe don't have much milk. / How much sugar?
MANYCountable plural❌ Negative / ❓ QuestionI don't know many people. / How many books?
SOMEBoth types✅ Positive / OffersThere are some apples. / Would you like some?
ANYBoth types❌ Negative / ❓ QuestionWe don't have any milk. / Do you have any?
A LOT OFBoth types✅ PositiveI have a lot of friends / a lot of money.
A FEWCountable onlyPositive (small number)A few people came. (not many but some)
FEWCountable onlyNegative meaningFew people stayed. (almost none)
A LITTLEUncountable onlyPositive (small amount)A little sugar is fine. (some)
LITTLEUncountable onlyNegative meaningLittle time remains. (almost none)

🔵 Countable vs Uncountable

  • Countable: books, friends, apples, people, questions, students
  • Uncountable: milk, water, money, sugar, time, information, advice, homework, news, furniture, luggage, experience
  • Use MUCH only with uncountable: much water ✓ / much books ✗
  • Use MANY only with countable: many books ✓ / many water ✗
  • SOME positive sentences + offers, ANY negatives + questions

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • I don't have many money.
    ✓ I don't have much money. (uncountable)
  • There are much apples.
    ✓ There are many apples. (countable)
  • I have a little friends.
    ✓ I have a few friends. (countable → few)
  • There is few milk left.
    ✓ There is little milk left. (uncountable → little)

Comparatives & Superlatives — المقارنة

Comparative للمقارنة بين شيئين، Superlative للتفضيل ضمن مجموعة. القاعدة الذهبية: لا تجمع صيغتي المقارنة.

Adjective TypeComparativeSuperlativeExample
Short (1 syllable): tall, long+ er + thanthe + esttaller than / the tallest
Ends in -y: easy, heavyy→i + ery→i + esteasier / the easiest
Long (2+ syllables): interestingmore + adj + thanthe most + adjmore interesting / the most interesting
Irregular: good / bad / farbetter / worse / fartherthe best / worst / farthestShe is the best student.
Ends in CVC: big, hotdouble + erdouble + estbigger / the biggest

💡 Key Rules

  • Always use than after comparative: taller than her sister ✓
  • Never double: more taller ✗ / more faster ✗
  • Superlative always needs the: the longest river ✓
  • good→better→best / bad→worse→worst (irregular)
  • as...as = equality: as tall as her brother
  • One of the most + adj + plural noun: one of the most beautiful cities
  • The more... the better = parallel comparison

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • She is more taller than me.
    ✓ She is taller than me.
  • He is the more good student.
    ✓ He is the best student.
  • She is taller then her sister.
    ✓ taller THAN (than ≠ then)
  • This is the most big building.
    ✓ This is the biggest building.

Prepositions — حروف الجر

حروف الجر المكانية والزمانية — أكثر نقاط الاختبار التي يخطئ فيها الطلاب. حفظ الجدول يضمن لك النجاح.

🕐 Time — الزمان

  • IN = months, years, seasons, parts of day: in January / in 2002 / in the morning
  • ON = specific days & dates: on Monday / on 5th May / on my birthday
  • AT = exact times & holidays & night: at 5 o'clock / at Christmas / at night
  • FOR = duration: for two weeks / for five years
  • SINCE = start point: since Monday / since 2015
  • DURING = within a period: during the holiday / during the meeting
  • BY = deadline: finish by Monday / arrive by 6 PM

📌 Place — المكان

  • IN = enclosed/cities/countries: in the park / in Cairo
  • ON = surfaces/floors: on the table / on the envelope / on the 2nd floor
  • AT = specific points: at the airport / at school / at the bus stop
  • NEAR = close to: my house is near the bank
  • OVER = above/across: the plane flew over the city
  • UNDER = below: the cat is under the table
  • BETWEEN = two things: between A and B
  • BY / NEXT TO = beside: sit by the window

🚗 Transport — وسائل النقل

  • BY + transport (no article): by car / by bus / by train / by plane / by bike
  • ON FOOT (not by foot): I went there on foot.
  • IN a specific car/taxi: She arrived in a taxi.
  • ON a bus/train/plane (public transport): She was on the bus.

Modal Verbs — أفعال الحالة

CAN/COULD/WOULD/MUST/SHOULD/MAY/MIGHT — القاعدة الذهبية: بعدها دائماً الفعل المجرد (base verb) بدون to.

ModalMain UsesExample
CANAbility / Permission (informal)He can speak three languages. / Can I leave early?
COULDPast ability / Polite request / PossibilityCould you help me, please? / She could swim at age 5.
WOULDPolite offers/requests / ConditionalWould you like some tea? / Would she like to go?
MUSTStrong obligation / Logical deductionYou must remember to post it. / He must be tired.
MUSTN'TProhibition (forbidden!)You mustn't park here. (لا يجوز)
SHOULDAdvice / RecommendationYou should study harder.
MAY / MIGHTPossibilityIt may/might rain tomorrow.
DON'T HAVE TONot necessary (NOT forbidden!)You don't have to come if you're busy.

💡 Key Rules

  • Modal + BASE VERB (no -s, no -ing, no to): He can speak ✓
  • No -s with he/she/it: He can ✓ / He cans ✗
  • MUST = own obligation / HAVE TO = external rule
  • MUSTN'T = forbidden ≠ DON'T HAVE TO = not necessary
  • Past ability: could (general) / was able to (specific moment)
  • Shall we...? = suggestion / Shall I...? = offer

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • You can going home now.
    ✓ You can go home. (base verb)
  • He cans speak English.
    ✓ He can speak English. (no -s)
  • Do you like some tea?
    ✓ Would you like some tea? (polite offer)
  • She could to swim at 5.
    ✓ She could swim at age 5. (no 'to')
🏗️

Special Structures — تراكيب خاصة

SO/SUCH/ENOUGH/TOO/Tag Questions/Conjunctions — تراكيب متكررة في الاختبار.

SO vs SUCH

  • SO + adjective (بدون اسم): The movie was so boring.
  • SUCH A + adj + singular noun: It was such a warm day.
  • SUCH + adj + plural noun: They are such beautiful places.
  • SO...THAT: It was so warm that we went swimming.
  • SUCH A...THAT: It was such a warm day that we went swimming.

ENOUGH & TOO

  • Adjective + enough: brave enough / tall enough / old enough
  • Enough comes AFTER adjectives but BEFORE nouns
  • Enough + noun: enough time / enough money
  • Too + adjective: too tired / too young (negative meaning)
  • Too + adj + to + base verb: too tired to work
  • Adj + enough + to + base verb: old enough to vote

Tag Questions — أسئلة الذيل

  • Positive → negative tag: She is tall, isn't she?
  • Negative → positive tag: He isn't here, is he?
  • They have finished, haven't they?
  • She will come, won't she?
  • Let's go... → shall we?
  • Open the door... → will you?

Conjunctions — روابط

  • AND = إضافة / BUT = تناقض
  • SO = نتيجة: She was tired, so she slept.
  • BECAUSE = سبب: She slept because she was tired.
  • ALTHOUGH = رغم: Although it rained, we went out.
  • HOWEVER = لكن (يبدأ جملة جديدة)
  • DESPITE + noun: Despite the rain, we went.

🔤 THIS / THAT / THESE / THOSE

Near — قريبFar — بعيد
SingularTHIS boy / bookTHAT girl / car
PluralTHESE books / peopleTHOSE cars / buildings

Conditionals — الجمل الشرطية

كل نوع له قاعدة صارمة تحكم الأزمنة المستخدمة. لا تستخدم will في جملة if.

TypeIf ClauseMain ClauseUseExample
ZeroIf + Present SimplePresent SimpleFacts / habitsIf you heat water to 100°C, it boils.
FirstIf + Present Simplewill + base verbFuture possibilityIf you study, you will pass.
SecondIf + Past Simplewould + base verbImaginary/unlikelyIf I had more time, I would learn French.
ThirdIf + Past Perfectwould have + V3Imaginary pastIf I had studied, I would have passed.

💡 Key Rules

  • في if/when/as soon as/until → لا نستخدم will: when I get home ✓
  • First: If + present → will. Second: If + past → would
  • In 2nd conditional: use were for all subjects: If I were you...
  • Unless = if not: Unless you study, you will fail.
  • يمكن تبديل الجملتين: I will pass if I study ✓

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • If I will study, I will pass.
    ✓ If I study, I will pass.
  • If I would have money, I buy it.
    ✓ If I had money, I would buy it.
  • If I was you, I would apologize.
    ✓ If I were you, I would apologize.

Phrasal Verbs — الأفعال المركبة

أفعال مركبة من فعل + حرف جر/ظرف. معناها الكامل يختلف عن مكوناتها. يجب حفظها كوحدة واحدة.

📋 المجموعة الأولى

  • Sign up for = التسجيل في: sign up for a course
  • Come up = أمر طارئ: Something has come up
  • Check in = تسجيل الوصول (فندق/مطار)
  • Check out = تسجيل المغادرة
  • Take out of = إخراج من: take out of the bag
  • Look at = النظر إلى / مراجعة
  • Come over = القدوم لزيارة
  • Turn up = الوصول/الظهور فجأة

📋 المجموعة الثانية

  • Look up = البحث في القاموس
  • Look after = الاعتناء بـ (care for)
  • Look for = البحث عن (search for)
  • Put off = التأجيل (postpone)
  • Give up = الاستسلام / التوقف عن
  • Find out = اكتشاف / معرفة
  • Run out of = نفاذ شيء
  • Break down = تعطل (machines)

📋 المجموعة الثالثة

  • Set up = تأسيس / تنظيم
  • Take off = إقلاع الطائرة / خلع
  • Turn on/off = تشغيل/إطفاء
  • Turn up/down = رفع/خفض الصوت
  • Pick up = رفع / تعلم بسرعة
  • Carry on = الاستمرار (continue)
  • Get on with = الانسجام مع
  • Hand in = تسليم (submit)

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • sign up tennis lessons
    ✓ sign up FOR tennis lessons
  • come over (for emergency)
    ✓ something has come UP
  • take the key out my bag
    ✓ take the key out OF my bag
  • looking after my keys
    ✓ looking FOR my keys

Articles — أدوات التعريف والتنكير

A / AN / THE / Ø — من أكثر القواعد التي يُخطأ فيها. القاعدة تعتمد على الصوت وليس الحرف.

ArticleUseExample
Aنكرة + صوت ساكن (consonant sound)a book / a university (يو) / a European
ANنكرة + صوت علة (vowel sound: a,e,i,o,u)an apple / an hour (h صامتة) / an honest man
THEمعرّف / وحيد / مذكور سابقاً / superlativethe capital / the Nile / the best / the Sun
Øجمع نكرة / مفاهيم عامة / أسماء بلاد/مدنChildren need love. / I live in Egypt.

💡 متى نستخدم THE؟

  • الأشياء الوحيدة: the sun / the moon / the capital city
  • مذكور سابقاً: "I saw a dog. The dog was big."
  • بعد superlatives: the most expensive / the best
  • الأنهار والمحيطات: the Nile / the Atlantic
  • الآلات الموسيقية: play the piano / the guitar
  • الدول الجمع: the United States / the Netherlands

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • What is a capital of France?
    ✓ What is THE capital? (unique)
  • She is an United States citizen.
    ✓ a United States citizen. (U = يو = ساكن)
  • He is a honest man.
    ✓ He is an honest man. (h صامتة)
  • I'm going to a Cairo.
    ✓ I'm going to Cairo. (أسماء مدن: لا أداة)
⚡

Adverbs — الظروف

الظروف تصف الأفعال والصفات. فهم الفرق بين Adjective و Adverb وترتيب Frequency Adverbs أساسي.

📋 Adjective vs Adverb

  • Adjective يصف الاسم: a slow car / a careful driver
  • Adverb يصف الفعل: drive slowly / work carefully
  • معظم Adverbs = Adjective + ly: slow→slowly, careful→carefully
  • استثناءات: fast→fast / hard→hard / late→late
  • استثناء مهم: good→well (ليس goodly!)
  • Linking verbs (feel/look/seem) → adjective: She looks beautiful

🔢 Frequency Adverbs — الموضع في الجملة

  • Always (100%) / Usually (80%) / Often (70%)
  • Sometimes (50%) / Rarely (20%) / Never (0%)
  • الموضع: قبل الفعل الرئيسي / بعد be / بعد الفعل المساعد
  • She is always happy. (after be)
  • He never smokes. (before main verb)
  • She has always been kind. (after auxiliary)

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Drive slow on this road.
    ✓ Drive SLOWLY. (adverb for verb)
  • He speaks English good.
    ✓ He speaks English well. (good→well)
  • She always is happy.
    ✓ She is always happy. (after be)
  • She sings beautiful.
    ✓ She sings beautifully.

💡 Degree Adverbs

  • very / extremely / quite / rather + adjective
  • deeply interested / highly recommended
  • clearly / obviously the best option
  • One of the most + adj + plural noun

Passive Voice — المبني للمجهول

نستخدمه عندما يكون الفاعل غير معروف أو غير مهم. الصيغة: be + V3. تتغير be حسب الزمن.

TenseActive VoicePassive Voice
Present SimpleThey make cars here.Cars are made here.
Past SimpleSomeone broke the window.The window was broken.
Present PerfectThey have built a new road.A new road has been built.
Future (will)They will deliver the package.The package will be delivered.
Modal VerbsYou must complete the form.The form must be completed.
Present ContinuousThey are building a mall.A mall is being built.

💡 Key Rules

  • الصيغة: Subject + be + V3 (past participle)
  • غيّر be حسب الزمن: is/are → was/were → has/have been → will be
  • الفاعل الأصلي بعد by: was written by Ahmed
  • إذا كان الفاعل غير معروف لا نذكره: My phone was stolen.
  • Modal Passive: must/should/can + be + V3

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • The cake was bake by my mother.
    ✓ The cake was baked. (V3)
  • English is speak all over the world.
    ✓ English is spoken. (V3)
  • It will delivered tomorrow.
    ✓ It will be delivered. (be + V3)
  • The form should prepared.
    ✓ The form should be prepared.
💬

Reported Speech — الكلام المنقول

لنقل كلام شخص آخر. القاعدة: نحرك الزمن للوراء خطوة واحدة عند النقل.

Direct Speech→Reported Speech (Tense Shift)
"I work here." (Pres. Simple)→She said she worked there.
"I am studying." (Pres. Cont.)→He said he was studying.
"I will help you." (will)→She said she would help me.
"I can swim." (can)→He said he could swim.
"I have finished." (Pres. Perf.)→She said she had finished.
"Go home." (Imperative)→She told me to go home.

💡 Time & Place Changes

  • now → then / today → that day
  • yesterday → the day before
  • tomorrow → the next day
  • here → there / this → that
  • Indirect questions: normal word order (no inversion)
  • Yes/No questions → if / whether

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • She said she is tired.
    ✓ She said she was tired. (shift back)
  • He said he will come tomorrow.
    ✓ He said he would come the next day.
  • She told me go home.
    ✓ She told me to go home.
  • He asked where is she.
    ✓ He asked where she was. (normal order)

Key Vocabulary — مفردات أساسية

مفردات متكررة في الاختبار مع شرح الفروق الدقيقة والتعبيرات الاصطلاحية.

🏢 Business & Work

  • Department = قسم: finance department
  • Brochure = كتيب دعائي (from travel agent)
  • Annual = سنوي: annual report/meeting
  • Restricted area = منطقة محظورة
  • Deadline = الموعد النهائي
  • Attend = يحضر (event) ≠ Visit = يزور (place)
  • Invoice = فاتورة / Receipt = إيصال

📝 Describing People

  • Frugal = مقتصد (careful with money)
  • Fickle = متقلب (changes mind often)
  • Reliable = موثوق / Determined = مصمّم
  • Athletic = رياضي البنية
  • Ambitious = طموح
  • Curly = مجعّد ≠ Straight = مستقيم
  • Huge = ضخم جداً (much bigger than 'big')

🔤 Confusing Words

  • Mind (do you mind?) = يمانع ≠ think
  • Lend = يُعير (من عنده) ≠ Borrow = يستعير
  • Say + شيء / Tell + شخص + شيء
  • Make a cake/decision ≠ Do homework/research
  • Raise (+ object) / Rise (no object)
  • Admire = يُعجب بـ / يحترم

🗣️ Key Expressions

  • In case = احتياطاً لـ: carry a card in case
  • Would like + obj + to + verb: She would like us to meet
  • Do you mind if...? = هل تمانع؟
  • I'm afraid... = للأسف / أخشى أن
  • So far = حتى الآن (Present Perfect)
  • It's worth + V-ing: worth visiting
  • I'd rather + base verb: I'd rather stay
  • Look forward to + V-ing: looking forward to seeing
